About Our Company

The "Empowering the Nation" is a small to mid-size enterprise (SME) whose main focus is on upskilling domestic workers and gardeners. The main aim is to create an easy-going, user-friendly platform that will advertise the business, allowing them to receive requests for information from potential customers for services requested.

Branding

The "Empowering the Nation" brand was designed by "Precious Radebe" in the year 2022 and offers courses in JHB. This platform offers six-week Short Skills Training programs and six-month longer Learnerships, hoping to empower and provide more marketable skills for domestic workers and gardeners.

Our Team

We are a diverse group of thinkers, creators, and doers. Our collective expertise allows us to tackle complex challenges and deliver impactful results. We are proud of the work we do and the communities we serve.
